Our Products & Services

WALL PANELS

Precision engineered in a controlled factory environment, panelized walls can be delivered flat-packed to site—reducing transportation cost and enabling fast on-site erection.

Tight manufacturing tolerances ensure an accurate, airtight envelope so internal comfort can be perfectly controlled. Homes can be supplied in numerous internal and external finishes, allowing full expression of your style.

ROOFS

Our roof structures provide a high-performance insulating barrier against harsh and variable climatic conditions. The roofing system acts as a comfort layer while also providing sound reduction across rooms, improving acoustic privacy throughout the home.
